MASSAGE THERAPY

Personalized Massage

Custom massage therapy incorporates a variety of modalities, or types of massage techniques, to ensure the best-individualized treatment. Using different combinations of massage, we are able to explore which modality is most beneficial for your health and wellness.

Sports Massage

A sports massage focuses on enhancing circulation, increasing tissue elasticity and reducing muscle tension. Sports massage is also used to help relieve tight muscles. Muscle tightness can limit flexibility, increase pain and cause future injuries.

Meridian massage

Meridian Massage is an ancient Chinese medical technique that incorporates various Chinese medicine and acupressure treatments to enhance health and relieve health issues. The soothing and relaxing massage is effective for treating sports injuries, stress, insomnia, migraines, as well as improving our digestion, blood and lymphatic flow. We stimulate the meridian lines within our body ‘paths’ where energy flows through. The paths are circulatory and exist in corresponding pairs circulating blood, nutrients, and energy throughout the body.
