ENERGY HEALING

Energy healing is a practice that harnesses the power of intention and subtle energy to facilitate healing and well-being, regardless of physical proximity. Using various techniques and modalities, healing energy will be directed to the client, addressing physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects to support their overall well-being. The sessions can be conducted over video calls, phone calls, or through a designated time set for the healing intention, allowing individuals to receive the benefits of healing energy from the comfort of their own location.
